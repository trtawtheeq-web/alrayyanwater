About Rayyan Water

Rayyan Natural Water Company was established in 1984 by Mr. Khalid bin Mohammed Al Rabban. From the very beginning, quality was Rayyan's goal to elevate the production process to a world-class level with technical assistance from the French company Evian. Rayyan is part of Al Rabban Holding Company, and is now a standalone, award-winning brand with high standards, a modern integrated facility with advanced laboratories, a fully hygienic production line, certified with international quality standards, and truly committed to the environment and community initiatives.

World-Class Bottling Nature First

At Rayyan Water, we are committed citizens of our community and truly dedicated to using the latest bottling and packaging technologies and eco-friendly manufacturing policies. We take all necessary and effective measures to reduce our environmental impact by carefully selecting the materials we use and working to minimize waste.

By adopting the latest technologies, we use the lightest packaging to reduce our carbon footprint. Even our highly efficient production systems contribute to reducing energy consumption.

Everything we do and every policy we adopt focuses on protecting and preserving nature by all possible means.
